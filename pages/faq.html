FAQ Page

  1. Who runs Evendar?

    Evendar is run by 3 UCSD students: Senyan Luo, Arya Kaul, and Hyerim Park. We developed Evendar to solve a problem we observed within UCSD, the lack of community. Our hope is that through Evendar, students will be able to expand their social bubble and get exposed to more of the wonderful events put on by student organizations around campus.

  2. How can I post an event?

    Currently, Evendar only allows events to be posted by registered student organizations on campus. If you are a board member of a student organization listed in UCSD's list of approved student organizations, click on the 'Hamburger' icon in the top right and click 'Post New Event' in the drop down menu. To prove your affiliation with the student organization, Evendar will pursue validation through the supplied club's email address. Then fill out the requisite information and we'll post it!

  3. Who will see my event?

    Everybody who uses Evendar will have the opportunity to see your event. Indeed, this freedom is a major reason we set out to develop Evendar. Using the Carousel View (Default View) you'll be treated to a shuffled list of events from a given week. We do not apply any fancy schmancy algorithms to pick which events we believe will best suit you, instead we treat you like the adult you are and let you pick what events sound interesting to you. Using the List View allows you to see all events in a given week chronologically.

  4. What's the catch?

    Nothing! We don't make ad revenue or monetize this information in anyway. We just love UCSD and want to see it grow in a positive manner!

  5. Who can post?

    Every registered student organization on campus has the ability to post. To create an event, simply click on the hamburger icon and select the 'Post Event | Edit Event' button. Sign up if you have not already created an account, and begin the signup procedure. If your organization already has an account, login with their credentials and post to your heart's content. If you need to change the login information for your student organization then send an email to evendar@gmail.com
